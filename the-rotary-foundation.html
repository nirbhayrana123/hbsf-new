    The Rotary Foundation

    Rotary Foundation (India) invests in seven global impact areas: promoting peace and conflict resolution, disease prevention and treatment, maternal and child health, basic education and literacy, water and sanitation, economic and community development, and environmental sustainability. 
    Implemented under Rotary Foundation (India), it mobilizes Rotary’s 175,000 members across 4,600+ clubs in India. It is an implementing agency for undertaking CSR activities in various sectors such as healthcare, education, livelihood, gender equity, etc.
    HBSF funding is used for accommodation and capital costs for an NGO that builds and operates shelters to house families of cancer-stricken children while they are undergoing treatment.  The funding is also given to rural areas for the improvement of Anganwadis. There are several other ongoing projects in various sectors.
