    SECTOR IN FOCUS

    Working across diverse sectors gives a nuanced understanding of each field and builds cross-sectoral learning, allowing us to respond with relevance.

    Education

    The sector’s potential lies in strengthening early learning and building robust institutions— enabling equitable access from early childhood to higher education, and advancing the vision set forth by the National Education Policy.

    Environment & Sustainability

    India faces growing environmental challenges, with air pollution and waste management affecting health, climate, and livelihoods. Addressing these matters calls for policy regulation, clean technologies and citizen-led action.

    Health

    Healthcare is among the largest areas of philanthropic investment, with growing focus on primary care, digital health, and data-driven solutions. Holistic approaches, inclusion, and mental well-being are emerging as vital priorities.

    Art and Culture

    With over 500,000 heritage sites and a wealth of art forms, this sector holds deep cultural and economic significance. Preserving traditions, nurturing skills, enabling access, and fostering new expressions are key to sustaining India's rich legacy and creative future.

    Economic Reforms

    Strengthening the foundation for lasting public goods requires sustained policy research, deep institutional collaboration, and a commitment to system-level thinking across sectors.

    Legal Aid

    Legal aid strives to provide social protection and accessible legal services. Grassroots efforts remain vital in reaching marginalized communities, while systemic reform offers pathways to long-term, inclusive, and transformative change.

    Livelihood

    Livelihoods are central to resilience and dignity. The focus lies in strengthening skills, entrepreneurship, and market access-especially for underserved communities while supporting systems that enable long-term income security.

    Sports

    Sport holds immense potential to drive social change-improving health, supporting school retention, advancing gender equality, and enhancing youth employability.Focused investment in talent development can connect community level participation to national and international sporting excellence.
