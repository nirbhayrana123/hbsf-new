    • “Some acts of care may never be seen, but they shift the world all the same”

    Note from the founders

    Some of the most meaningful moments in our lives have come from simply being there
    for someone—listening, learning, and sharing what we could.

    That instinct is what led us to start this foundation.

    Philanthropy will always be about choice: the choice to stand beside people doing
    important work, to rethink norms where needed, and to let responsibility and compassion
    guide the way.

    As you walk your journey—whether in giving, creating, or caring—be free with your ideas
    and your heart because lasting change comes from the sum of all our deeds.

    Bina and Harish Shah

    About Us

    In the early 2000s, we began supporting efforts that opened
    up access to education, healthcare, and dignified livelihoods.

    What started as personal giving grew into long-term
    relationships with grassroots organizations, and eventually into
    a foundation built to carry this work forward with care,
    patience, and commitment.
