    Our Collabrations

    Our collaborations are designed to build trust, sharpen strategy, and hold space for learning and leadership.

    Accelerate Indian Philanthropy

    Accelerate Indian Philanthropy builds a vibrant network of philanthropists to inspire and inform strategic giving. As a core funder, HBSF supports its convenings, knowledge creation, partnerships, and advocacy to strengthen philanthropy in India.

    Dasra

    HBSF partners with Dasra, a philanthropy advisory firm, on a multi-year program supporting nonprofits across diverse geographies and communities. cohort includes organizations working in education, health, arts & culture, and social empowerment.

    India Leaders for Social Sector

    HBSF partners with India Leaders for Social Sector through the "Harish & Bina Shah Centre for Talent and Leadership", to strengthen leadership capacity across India. The Centre builds values-driven leadership through learning, convenings, and knowledge creation.

    Sattva

    In partnership with HBSF, Sattva, a development consultancy, runs a multi-year grant program providing secure funding and strengthening core capabilities of nonprofits across India. Sectors include education, health, livelihoods, legal aid, waste management and air quality.

    The Convergence Foundation

    HBSF is a founding partner of the “Economic Growth Collaborative”, an initiative of The Convergence Foundation. This partnership includes organizations working on reforms in exports, science and technology, participation, sustainable livelihoods and tourism.
